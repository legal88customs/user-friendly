# User Friendly
*A harm reduction app for overdose prevention, community support, and disease reduction.*

---

## Overview
User Friendly is a harm reduction app designed to **prevent overdoses and reduce the spread of disease**. It connects individuals with harm reduction spotters, nearby resources, and educational materials to promote safety and health. Our mission is to empower people to make safer choices and access critical resources without stigma.

---

## Features
- **Overdose Prevention Support**  
  Connect with trained spotters who can monitor and respond in case of emergencies.
- **Community Connections**  
  Connect with other users and harm reduction spotters for real-time support.
- **Resource Locator**  
  Find nearby harm reduction services such as:
  - Safe consumption sites
  - Needle exchanges
  - Health clinics
- **Education & Guidance**  
  Access harm reduction tips, best practices, and verified information.
- **Privacy & Security**  
  Built with user safety and confidentiality in mind.
